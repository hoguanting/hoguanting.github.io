<h2>TeaMWork 2024</h2>

I joined [BDO](https://www.bdo.my/) as an intern in 2024 to work on this project. I collaborated with a team of 6 students from [Monash Warwick Alliance](https://www.linkedin.com/company/monash-warwick-alliance/posts/?feedView=all) to develop a CSR gamification platform for BDO internal use. We conducted a comprehensive analysis and stakeholder requirements studies for internal CSR platform redesign. We designed a new gamified user experience by researching competing platforms and creating a low-fidelity prototype in Figma. We defined the project's technical scope, roadmap, and budget for the new wellness-focused gamified platform, presenting the final feasibility report and technical specifications to BDO management. Source available on [GitHub](https://github.com/hoguanting/ThoughFull-CSR-Gamification).
