I'm a software engineer focused on building fast, reliable, and user-centric applications. I studied computer science at [Monash University](https://www.monash.edu/), where I spent time exploring how tools shape the way we think.

Right now, I'm particularly drawn to fintech and productivity products, especially the kind of consumer platforms that are part of everyday life — from payments to daily errands. I like thinking about how software gets built end-to-end, from understanding what users actually need to shipping something that feels simple and reliable in their hands.

Outside of work, I'm usually exploring coffee shops around Kuala Lumpur, reading science fiction, or reading up on urban design and how cities are shaped over time — [Perdana Botanical Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Perdana_Botanical_Gardens) is probably my favorite spot in the city for that kind of thinking.
